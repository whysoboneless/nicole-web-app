CPA Network Integration Guide

What is a CPA Network?

CPA (Cost Per Action) networks connect advertisers with publishers. When someone clicks your link and completes an action (sign-up, purchase, etc.), you get paid.

Popular CPA Networks: Glitchy, MaxBounty, CPAlead, OfferVault, PeerFly

Finding Your Offer ID

Step 1: Log into Your CPA Network

Go to your CPA network dashboard (e.g., Glitchy.ai, MaxBounty.com)

Step 2: Browse Available Offers

Look for sections like:

  • "Offers" or "Offer Wall"
  • "Available Offers"
  • "Marketplace"

Step 3: Find the Offer ID

The offer ID is usually displayed as:

  • Offer ID: Usually a number (e.g., 12345)
  • Offer Code: Sometimes an alphanumeric code
  • Look in the offer details or URL when you click "Get Link"
Example: Offer ID: 160550

Creating Your Tracking URL

Method 1: From CPA Network Dashboard

  1. Click on the offer you want to promote
  2. Click "Get Link" or "Generate Tracking Link"
  3. Copy the generated tracking URL
  4. This URL already includes your affiliate ID and tracking parameters

Method 2: Manual URL Construction

Some networks require you to build the URL manually:

https://network.com/offer/[OFFER_ID]?aff_id=[YOUR_AFFILIATE_ID]&sub1=[TRACKING_ID]

Replace [OFFER_ID] with your offer ID, [YOUR_AFFILIATE_ID] with your affiliate ID from the network, and optionally add sub1 for campaign tracking.

Glitchy.ai Specific Instructions

  1. Log into your Glitchy account
  2. Go to the "Offers" section
  3. Browse available offers or use the search
  4. Click on an offer to see details
  5. Find the Offer ID in the offer details (usually shown as a number)
  6. Click "Get Link" or "Generate Tracking Link"
  7. Copy the full tracking URL provided

Example Glitchy Offer ID:

160550

This would appear in your referral link: https://app.glitchy.ai/sign-up?rid=160550

Adding to Nicole AI

Step 1: Go to Products

Navigate to Products in the sidebar

Step 2: Add Product

Click "Add Product" and fill in:

  • Product Name: Name of the offer/product
  • Product URL: The original product URL (not tracking URL)
  • CPA Network: e.g., "Glitchy"
  • CPA Offer ID: The offer ID from your network (e.g., 160550)
  • Tracking URL: Your full tracking link from the network

Step 3: Use in Campaigns

When creating a campaign, select your saved product. Nicole will automatically use your tracking URL for all promotions.

Troubleshooting

Can't find Offer ID?

Check the URL when you click "Get Link" - the offer ID is often in the URL parameters

Tracking URL not working?

Make sure you copied the full URL including all parameters. Test it in an incognito window.

Need help with a specific network?

Check your network's documentation or contact their support. Each network has different link formats.
